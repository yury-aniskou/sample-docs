# What vaccines does my dog need?

Core vaccines recommended for all dogs include rabies, distemper, parvovirus, and adenovirus (hepatitis), often combined in the DHPP vaccine. Non-core vaccines like Bordetella, Leptospirosis, and Lyme disease are recommended based on your dog's lifestyle and geographic location.

# How often does my dog need a rabies vaccine?

Puppies receive their first rabies vaccine at 12–16 weeks of age. A booster is given one year later, then every 1–3 years depending on your local laws and the vaccine brand used. Rabies vaccination is required by law in most US states.

# What is the DHPP vaccine for dogs?

DHPP is a combination vaccine protecting against four diseases: Distemper, Hepatitis (Adenovirus), Parvovirus, and Parainfluenza. It is considered a core vaccine given to puppies in a series starting at 6–8 weeks of age, with boosters every 3–4 weeks until 16 weeks, then annually or every 3 years.
