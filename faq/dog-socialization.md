# How do I introduce my dog to other dogs?

Introduce dogs on neutral ground, both on a loose leash. Allow them to approach each other naturally and watch for relaxed body language (loose tail wag, play bow). Separate them if you see stiff posture, prolonged staring, or growling. Keep the first interaction short and positive, gradually increasing duration.

# How do I introduce my dog to a new baby?

Before the baby arrives, play recordings of infant sounds to desensitize your dog, establish boundaries, and update obedience training. When the baby comes home, let your dog sniff a blanket with the baby's scent first, then allow calm, supervised introductions. Never leave a dog and infant unsupervised.

# How do I help my dog get along with cats?

Introduce dog and cat slowly, keeping them separated at first with scent swapping using bedding. Use a baby gate or crack in the door for visual contact before direct interaction. Always give the cat escape routes and high perches. Keep the dog on a leash during initial face-to-face meetings and reward calm behavior.
