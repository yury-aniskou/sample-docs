# How often should I bathe my dog?

Most dogs need a bath every 4–6 weeks, though this varies by breed, coat type, and activity level. Dogs with oily coats like Basset Hounds may need weekly baths, while those with water-repellent coats like Labrador Retrievers need less frequent bathing. Always use a dog-specific shampoo.

# How do I brush my dog's teeth?

Use a dog-specific toothbrush and toothpaste — never human toothpaste, as it contains xylitol which is toxic to dogs. Start by letting your dog taste the toothpaste, then gently brush in circular motions along the gum line. Aim for daily brushing; a minimum of three times per week is recommended.

# How often should I trim my dog's nails?

Dog nails should typically be trimmed every 3–4 weeks. You can tell it's time when you hear clicking on hard floors. Cut only the curved tip, avoiding the "quick" — the pink vascular core — which will bleed and cause pain if cut. If you're unsure, ask your vet or groomer to demonstrate.
