# What is the most popular dog breed in the United States?

The French Bulldog has been the most popular dog breed in the United States since 2022, surpassing the Labrador Retriever which held the top spot for 31 consecutive years. French Bulldogs are prized for their adaptability to apartment living and affectionate temperament.

# What are the best dog breeds for families with children?

Breeds known for their gentle, patient temperament with children include Golden Retrievers, Labrador Retrievers, Beagles, Boxers, and Cavalier King Charles Spaniels. Temperament depends heavily on individual training and socialization in addition to breed tendencies.

# What is the difference between a purebred and a mixed-breed dog?

A purebred dog has parents of the same breed and a documented lineage, offering predictable traits and appearance. A mixed-breed dog has parents of different breeds, often resulting in a unique combination of traits. Mixed-breed dogs are sometimes healthier due to greater genetic diversity.
